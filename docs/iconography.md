# Iconography Manifest — what each plate depicts

The creative direction for all 81 plates. Authors follow these depictions; small
compositional judgment is yours, the symbol is not. "Accent" names the one accent
element (see style guide).

## Characters (medallions, `emblems/characters/`)

- **jose-arcadio-buendia** — a magnifying glass focusing sun-rays toward a tiny adobe house; a loop of rope rests at the base. Accent: the focused ray-point.
- **ursula** — a lit candle ringed by a procession of tiny candy roosters and fishes. Accent: the flame.
- **jose-arcadio** — a full-rigged ship over wave-lines, a tattooed anchor beneath (tattoo marks as tiny glyph-like scratches, not letters). Accent: the anchor's rope.
- **colonel-aureliano-buendia** — one little fish centered, ringed by exactly 32 short radiating strokes; a faint chalk circle (dashed) around it. Accent: the fish, gold-filled.
- **amaranta** — a hand wrapped in a gauze bandage holding a threaded needle; a hem of shroud stitches runs along the lower frame. Accent: the thread.
- **rebeca** — a small canvas sack tied with cord, spilling a little earth; beside it a worn spoon. Accent: trickle of earth.
- **arcadio** — a schoolmaster's ruler crossed with a rifle; a blindfold ribbon drapes over the crossing. Accent: the ribbon.
- **aureliano-jose** — a domino tile pierced clean through by a bullet hole, crack-lines radiating. Accent: the bullet hole's rim.
- **seventeen-aurelianos** — seventeen small ash crosses in a loose arc, one slightly apart. Accent: the one apart.
- **remedios-the-beauty** — two bedsheets billowing upward with motion ticks, rising toward a plain sun disk. Accent: the sun.
- **jose-arcadio-segundo** — a fighting rooster perched on a boxcar wheel; faint tally-marks hatch the shadow. Accent: the rooster's comb.
- **aureliano-segundo** — an accordion mid-stretch with banknotes fluttering out of it. Accent: one banknote.
- **meme** — a clavichord (keyboard seen at angle) with a single butterfly resting on the keys. Accent: the butterfly.
- **jose-arcadio-the-seminarian** — a papal tiara reflected upside-down in a still pool (reflection dashed). Accent: tiara's center jewel.
- **amaranta-ursula** — an open birdcage, door swung wide, two swallows out and banking. Accent: the cage door.
- **aureliano-babilonia** — an open parchment with cipher-lines (short abstract strokes, not letters), a quill laid across, books stacked beneath. Accent: quill nib.
- **the-last-aureliano** — a small spiral (a pig's tail curl) being carried off by a column of ants. Accent: the spiral.
- **melquiades** — an alchemist's flask with vapor curling into a script-like wisp; a squat inkpot and parchment corner behind. Accent: the vapor wisp.
- **pilar-ternera** — a fanned deck of cards, face-down backs patterned with suns/moons; one card turned showing a heart shape. Accent: the heart.
- **santa-sofia-de-la-piedad** — an oil lamp and a broom crossed at a doorway's threshold line. Accent: lamp flame.
- **visitacion** — a knotted hammock end and a small overturned crown at its foot. Accent: crown rim.
- **cataure** — a traveler's bundle on a stick, a river reed leaning against it. Accent: the bundle knot.
- **remedios-moscote** — a child's rag doll seated on a folded bridal veil. Accent: doll's dress.
- **pietro-crespi** — an opened music box, its spring sprung outward in a long spiral. Accent: the spring.
- **fernanda-del-carpio** — a chamber pot embossed with a crowned crest, set on a lace doily. Accent: the crest.
- **petra-cotes** — a raffle wheel, and around its rim a multiplying run of rabbits (three, then five, suggested). Accent: wheel pointer.
- **mauricio-babilonia** — a mechanic's wrench with a swarm of butterflies rising off it. Accent: the swarm (tight group counts as one accent).
- **gaston** — a biplane propeller standing upright beside a velocipede wheel. Accent: propeller hub.
- **nigromanta** — a cooking pot, steam curling into a crescent; a tally of debts scratched beside (abstract ticks). Accent: the crescent of steam.
- **apolinar-moscote** — a government decree, seal dangling by a ribbon, pinned by a paperweight. Accent: the wax seal.
- **gerineldo-marquez** — a telegraph key beside a chess knight. Accent: the key's contact point.
- **general-moncada** — a pair of spectacles resting atop two crossed, surrendered pistols. Accent: one lens glint.
- **prudencio-aguilar** — a fighting rooster drawn entirely in the ghost convention (dashed), an esparto wisp at its feet. Accent: none in fill; accent stroke on the wisp.
- **francisco-the-man** — a walking staff and a hung song-scroll whose lines are drawn as wavering strokes. Accent: scroll ribbon.
- **catarino** — a tavern lantern and a felt flower. Accent: the flower.
- **father-nicanor** — a cup of hot chocolate levitating a hand's-width above its saucer, motion ticks beneath. Accent: the chocolate's surface.
- **father-antonio-isabel** — a confessional lattice with an ash cross thumb-printed at its center. Accent: none gold; accent stroke on cross.
- **mr-herbert** — a banana laid on a jeweler's balance, calipers beside. Accent: the banana.
- **mr-brown** — a screened bungalow behind a wire fence, a small fan on the porch. Accent: fence insulator knobs.
- **the-wise-catalonian** — a listing stack of books tied with string, a quill standing in an inkpot on top. Accent: the string.
- **gabriel** — a strapped suitcase with one thick book lashed under the strap; a paper airplane departs above. Accent: the paper plane.

## Places (plates, `emblems/places/`)

- **buendia-house** — long facade with a begonia porch, the chestnut tree at the left gable; one wing visibly newer than the other. Accent: begonias.
- **melquiades-room** — a work table with inkstand and stacked parchments under a high window's light-shaft (light as sparse straight rays). Accent: the inkstand.
- **the-workshop** — a goldsmith's bench: crucible, tongs, and a row of six tiny fishes. Accent: the row of fishes.
- **the-chestnut-tree** — the great tree, a rope loop around its trunk, a small stool beneath; a dashed bird overhead (ghost convention). Accent: none gold; accent stroke on rope.
- **the-begonia-porch** — porch railing with potted begonias, two rocking chairs angled together, a sewing basket. Accent: begonias.
- **macondo** — a row of adobe houses by a river of polished stones, almond trees between, sierra behind. Accent: the sun above the sierra.
- **the-church** — small whitewashed facade, bell in its arch, a coin tilted at the door. Accent: the bell.
- **catarinos-store** — a counter with bottles, a hung lantern, an accordion resting at the end. Accent: lantern flame.
- **street-of-the-turks** — a run of awninged shopfronts, bolts of cloth hung out, a macaw on a perch. Accent: the macaw.
- **the-river** — clear water over large polished stones "like prehistoric eggs" — make the stones ovoid and monumental. Accent: one stone's highlight.
- **the-cemetery** — an iron gate arch, small leaning crosses, one grand sepulcher; diagonal rain hatching. Accent: a single flower at one cross.
- **the-train-station** — a canopied platform, the innocent yellow train arriving with smoke curl. Accent: the locomotive's cowcatcher or boiler front.
- **the-banana-compound** — bungalows in a row behind wire fence on white posts; a sprinkler's arc; one banana leaf big in the foreground. Accent: the leaf's midrib.
- **the-zoological-brothel** — paper lanterns strung between posts, a heron standing tall, a tortoise below. Accent: one lantern.
- **the-catalonians-bookshop** — sagging shelves, books in string-tied bundles on the floor, a ladder. Accent: the string.
- **riohacha** — a colonial watchtower over the sea, a pirate ship (dashed — a story, not a sight) on the horizon. Accent: tower beacon.
- **the-swamp** — mangrove roots in mist (mist as broken horizontal strokes), a conquistador's breastplate half-sunk. Accent: none gold; accent stroke on breastplate rim.
- **the-spanish-galleon** — a galleon hull aground in flowering jungle, orchids on the rigging. Accent: the orchids.
- **neerlandia** — a great flowering ceiba over a small table with an inkstand and a single sheet. Accent: the ceiba's flowers.
- **the-highland-city** — a gloomy skyline of many bell towers under long diagonal rain; streets empty. Accent: one lit window.
- **europe** — a steamship prow passing under an old-world arcade of arches; pigeons. Accent: the ship's flag.

## Motifs (medallions, `emblems/motifs/`)

- **yellow-butterflies** — one large butterfly, wings patterned with fine hatching, two small ones behind. Accent: large wings' fill.
- **little-gold-fishes** — a fish with visible scale-work, hung from a delicate chain. Accent: the fish.
- **ice** — a rough block with an inner star of fracture lines, held by tongs. Accent: the star.
- **the-parchments** — a scroll unrolling, its lines abstract cipher strokes; a key laid on it. Accent: the key.
- **insomnia-and-forgetting** — a wide-open eye under a crescent moon; below, a blank hanging tag on a string (the labels on things). Accent: the moon.
- **solitude** — a single lit candle very small at center, vast empty field around it (the negative-space plate — break the 55–70% rule deliberately). Accent: the flame.
- **the-pigs-tail** — a tight spiral curl alone at center, a fine genealogical line descending into it from the frame's top. Accent: none gold; accent stroke on the spiral.
- **yellow-flowers** — small flowers falling at a drift angle, gathering in a low drift. Accent: the flowers (group).
- **ghosts** — a rocking chair drawn solid, its occupant's outline dashed. Accent: none.
- **circular-time** — a clock face whose two hands bend into a circle chasing each other; tick marks slightly irregular. Accent: the hands.
- **alchemy** — an alembic dripping into a small pan holding a bright bead. Accent: the bead.
- **the-ants** — a column of ants crossing the full field diagonally, carrying leaf fragments and one tiny spiral. Accent: the spiral.

## Eras (banners, `emblems/eras/`)

- **founding** — a caravan (packs, a birdcage on a pole, figures from behind) crossing a sierra pass toward a river valley; compass-rose sun. Accent: the sun.
- **insomnia** — a moonlit street where every object bears a small blank tag on a string. Accent: the moon.
- **wars** — rifles stacked in a pyramid, a torn banner, a drum; along the bottom inner frame run 32 small tick marks. Accent: the banner.
- **banana** — a train crossing on a trestle, telegraph wires dipping alongside, banana bunches hung in the foreground. Accent: the bananas.
- **deluge** — long diagonal rain over sagging rooftops; a fish swims down the middle of the street. Accent: the fish.
- **wind** — parchment pages caught in a great spiral of wind lines that tightens toward the right edge. Accent: one page.
